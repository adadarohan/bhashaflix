Numbers

When learning a language, any language, it is important to know how to write and say numerical values. Whether you are ordering something or stating the number of items you have, you are likely to need to know how to say or write numbers. The numbers in Tulu are relatively easy to write down. In fact, they resemble the numbers in Hindi.
