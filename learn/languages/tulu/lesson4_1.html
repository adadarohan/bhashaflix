Common Nouns

What are nouns? Nouns are words are used to identify a name, place, animal or thing. It is needless to say that they are extremely important to know, atleast a handful, of basic nouns. If visiting a place where Tulu is the main language, by knowing a good amount of basic nouns, you will be able to refer to something you want or a place you want to get to and will be able to get guidance from a local.
